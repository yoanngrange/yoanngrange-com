Meet Crisp Founders

Published on 26/08/2020

How Crisp hypergrowth has been reached during Covid pandemic without doing much than having a good product and a good product-market fit. Discover Crisp presented by its founders.

Their 10 people team and platform serve millions of conversations from hundreds of thousands of clients through many channels with a simple app which codebase is rewritten every two years. No product manager, no QA, no Sales. Fixing bugs ASAP, doing support, rewriting the whole product every two years, and delivering great value is what Crisp is made of.
